# Course Design Draft

This draft transfers the Spring 2026 PH 142 architecture into Summer Session D, July 6 to August 14, 2026.

## Confirmed From The Official Listing

- PBHLTH 142, section 001, lecture 001, class number 13581
- 4 units
- Online instruction
- Monday through Friday, 9:30 to 10:59 AM Pacific
- Labs are online and synchronous
- Lectures are online with a mix of synchronous and asynchronous meetings
- Enrollment cap is 260

## Design Assumptions

- Keep the Spring intellectual sequence: data/R, visualization, descriptive statistics, regression basics, categorical tables, study design, probability, distributions, CLT, inference, t tests, ANOVA, proportions, regression inference, chi-square, and review.
- Treat "one month" as the official six-week Summer Session D.
- Use the daily 90-minute lecture slot for the main course spine.
- Use one midterm and one final exam for Summer pacing.
- Preserve the Spring assignment architecture but compress cadence: 6 weekly quizzes, 10 problem sets plus onboarding, 11 lab assignments, and 3 data project milestones.
- Keep labs synchronous and online. Sections 101A (#13583) and 102A (#13584) meet Monday through Thursday from 11:00 to 11:59 AM Pacific; sections 103A (#13585) and 104A (#13586) meet Monday through Thursday from 5:00 to 5:59 PM Pacific.
- Use Ed Discussion, not Piazza, for course questions and announcements.
- Treat bonus and review material as flexible: it can be synchronous, asynchronous, or folded into lab/review sessions depending on instructor preference.
